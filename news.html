ChiroKumar got beaten by Biker

Recently , joint supplier of MIST CS got beaten up pretty bad by random biker. The story goes kind of like this.

On a sunday , our respected joint supplier , Mr. faisal was riding his bike to his school in the morning . As he had a class test at first period he was in a bit rush.

On the বাউনিয়া চৌরাস্তা, approximately at 7:22 AM , an accident happened. Mr. Rayhan was trying to be a little adventurous on the road. Now if you ask why he was trying to be adventurous on a busy road, let me tell you the reason . He unfortunately doesn't get the oppertunity to adventurous on batch trips , because batch trips doesn't happen in his class.

Because most of his batchmates are comfortable using the excuse " দোস্ত বাসা থেকে যাইতে দিবে না" instead of actually going on batch trips (You are welcome if you get offended by this )

After rayhan bhai was 2 seconds away from being DOM from FAST AND FURIOUS franchise, he collided with a biker . As rayhan bhai was aware of the fact that the fault was his , he tried to escape, but the biker started swearing as a typical bengali biker.

After hearing all those vulgar comments about his family from the biker, rayhan again lost his temper and asked the biker politely

"ঐ মিয়া বাইক চালাইতে না পারলে রাস্তায় নামসেন ক্যান ?"

After 2 seconds, rayhan bhai was seen on বাউনিয়া roads running for his life , and the biker bhai on his tail with a rod . After a short session of "আদব-কায়দা শেখানো" rayhan bhai returned to MIST , almost unharmed.....
